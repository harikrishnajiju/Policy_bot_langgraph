Employee Expense and Reimbursement Policy

General Policy
Employees will be reimbursed for reasonable and necessary expenses incurred while conducting company business. All expenses must be submitted through the "ExpensePro" online portal within 30 days of the expense being incurred.

Submitting Expenses
A clear, itemized receipt is required for all expenses over $25. For expenses under $25, a receipt is not required, but you must list the vendor and purpose.

Travel and Meals
Flights must be booked in economy class. Hotel accommodations should be at a standard, mid-range hotel. When traveling, employees are entitled to a daily meal per diem of $75.

Client Entertainment
Reasonable expenses for client meals and entertainment are reimbursable. You must include the names of all attendees and the business purpose of the meeting in your expense report. Expenses for alcoholic beverages must be submitted as a separate line item.
